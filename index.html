Welcome to Nymbul's Webpage

Recently, as I've been expanding my knowledge in computers, I've realized a love for science, math, data, and really the true depth of what "computer science" is.

Fortunatenly and unfortunately, I don't think I will feel satisfied with anything but complete knowledge. As I've delved deeper into each subsection of computer science, I noticed a real reliance on pre-built tools and pre-discovered knowledge to meet whatever experimental ends I was attempting to accomplish. I appreciate being a techy, but an imposter syndrome has replaced my confidence with time.

What is this page for?

I need a place to document thoughts, projects, experiments, knowledge... a hub to organize myself and track progress in this field. I can't keep throwing myself at things, learning only a quarter of the material and building hacked together projects that work in only the specific way I know how to use them. It's not scalable. This could be a portfolio in the future.

Why is this webpage so ugly?

Because it's css file is blank, I haven't learned css very well yet and don't intend to take shortcuts. Copy and pasting, frameworks, even templates feel like cheating at this point to me. I feel the only way to progress and feel confident is to build each function independently and document the process.

How will this site be layed out?

Well, welcome to the main page for now. Projects will appear in a blog format under a blog page. I will need seperate blog categories for general programming, personal, gaming, philosophy, mathematics, hacking, and maybe even some secret ones only accessible through capture the flag style "gameplay". I am not a designer, so I will need to take some time with this.
